Common Questions

When will I get my 50% advance salary?

After your registration and fee verification, the 50% advance salary will be credited to your bank account or UPI ID within 5 to 10 minutes.

How do I complete the registration?

Fill out the application form below, scan the QR code to pay the project fee, and send the payment screenshot to us on WhatsApp +91 9983251041.

How do I receive the project materials?

The writing parcel (A4 sheets and book content) will be sent to your registered address via DTDC or BlueDart courier within 24 hours of registration.

When and how will I get the remaining 50% salary?

The final payment is released instantly once you finish the work and share the photos of the completed pages for final verification.

Who will collect the completed work?

Our courier executive will visit your home at the end of the project duration to pick up the written sheets. There are no extra charges for pickup.
